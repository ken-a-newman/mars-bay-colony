What might a Funeral on Mars look like?

To drop the Grim references, the Deceased will be referred to as "The Explorer"

The Explorer would be sealed and preserved in a cigar shaped Sarcophagus referred here as the "Pod" with a small window if requested.

A Rocket Vehicle would be launched from Earth with the Explorer, Pod, several Robots, and a light time capsule.

Upon Landing in a chosen location on Mars, an Earthbound Funeral director would begin working with Avatar Drivers and the Robots to prepare the Ceremony.

The Pod would be arranged to take advantage of a visually spectacular backdrop

A large Monitor and Speakers would be arranged in front of the Pod to provide a Visual and Audible Broadcast of the service from Earth.

Cameras would be arranged to capture live the service including the Pod, Monitor, and Backdrop and Broadcast from Mars to Earth

The ceremony would take place simutaneously on Earth and Mars with the signals flowing in both directions - with some measure to prevent a feedback loop.

The Explorer may elect to make the Signal from Mars available to all - or just to Family
