How would Art from Mars work?

A Rocket would be launched from Earth to Mars delivering a pair of very special Robots to be used as Avatars.

One Robot would be named Michelangelo, the other we'll leave open for now.

The landing site will be chosen for offering a collection of stones of varying height and girth. Perfect for sculpting. We will also be looking for a good natural backdrop for the sculptures.

An Earthbound sculptor of considerable reputation will be selected and trained to operate The Michelangelo Avatar. A suitable stone will be slected and the Sculptor via Avatar will begin the work.

Once the Sculpture is completed, a dramatic and high quality Signed multi panel Lithograph will be produced.



The second Robot will be skilled in the Art of "Sand Drawing". The same as the Sculptor, an Earthbound Avatar Artist will create magnificent Sand Sculptures which will be captured in Signed Lithograph form. If the wind don't blow.
